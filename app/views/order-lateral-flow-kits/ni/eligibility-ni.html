In Northern Ireland, free COVID-19 testing is no longer available for most people. You can only order rapid lateral flow tests if:

  • you may be eligible for COVID-19 treatments (opens in new tab)
  • you are working or visiting others in high risk or vulnerable settings, such as care homes and hospital environments
  • you are a carer who provides close personal care for someone who is at higher risk if they contract COVID-19
  • your GP or a healthcare professional asks you to take a test

Find out what to do if you think you have COVID-19 (opens in new tab). If you're worried, or your symptoms get worse, contact your GP or your GP out of hours service. In an emergency dial 999.
