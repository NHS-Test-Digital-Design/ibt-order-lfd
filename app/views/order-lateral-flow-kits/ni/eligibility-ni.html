In Northern Ireland, you can order rapid lateral flow tests if:

  • you have COVID-19 symptoms (opens in new tab)
  • you think you may be eligible for COVID-19 treatments (opens in new tab)
  • your GP or a healthcare professional has asked you to take a test
  • you're working in or visiting high risk or vulnerable settings, for example care homes and hospital environments
  • you're a carer who provides close personal care for someone who is at higher risk if they get COVID-19

Find out what to do if you think you have COVID-19. If you're worried, or your symptoms get worse, contact your GP or your GP out of hours service. In an emergency dial 999.
