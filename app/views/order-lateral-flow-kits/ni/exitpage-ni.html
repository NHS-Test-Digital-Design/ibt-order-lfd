Do not phone for a test. You will not be able to get one this way.

You may be able to get a free test from a pharmacy if you are:

  • eligible for COVID-19 treatments
  • a carer who provides close personal care for someone who is at higher risk from COVID-19 and you have COVID-19 symptoms

If you work in health or social care

You may still be able to get free lateral flow tests through your employer.

If you have symptoms

Find out what to do if you think you have COVID-19. If you're worried, or your symptoms get worse, contact your GP or your GP out-of-hours service. In an emergency, dial 999.
